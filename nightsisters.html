Nightsisters

Asajj Lead

Difficulty

  • A
  • B
  • C
  • D

Team Details

Infinite revives potential with Nightsister Zombie, damage through protection, healing, and cleansing.

Important Zetas

  • Asajj Ventress: Nightsister Swiftness (Lead)
  • Asajj Ventress: Rampage (Unique)
  • Old Daka: Serve Again (Unique)

Speed / Mods Checks

  • Asajj Ventress (Speed)
  • Old Daka (Health)
  • Mother Talzin (Offense)

Kill Order

  1. Old Daka
  2. Asajj Ventress
  3. Mother Talzin
  4. Everyone Else
  5. Nightsister Zombie

Hard Counters

Should work every time as long as gear levels are not far off and no huge mod disadvantages.
